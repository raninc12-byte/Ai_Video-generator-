## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the buttons!"
- Make sure the window is **not minimized**
- Look for the **RED and GREEN buttons** in the middle
- They're big and colorful!

### "Nothing happens when I click!"
1. Make sure you typed a prompt
2. Click **GENERATE** button (red at bottom)
3. Not the color selection buttons

### "Both buttons show ✓ "
- Only one can be selected at a time
- Click the one you want
- Its checkmark will appear

### "Generating takes forever"
- **First time:** Downloads AI model (normal 1-2 minutes)
- **Smooth mode:** 2-4 minutes (includes interpolation)
- **Quick mode:** 1-3 minutes
- This is normal!
